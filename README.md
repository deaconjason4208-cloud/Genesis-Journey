# Genesis Journey

**Genesis Journey** is a faith-driven Bible adventure game that takes players through the story of Scripture from **Genesis to Revelation**. Players choose their path, answer Bible-based questions, face challenges inspired by biblical events, and grow through a story-rich spiritual journey.

Built to be engaging, challenging, and meaningful, Genesis Journey combines adventure gameplay, biblical learning, and immersive progression into one experience.

---

## Overview

Genesis Journey is designed as an interactive Christian adventure game where players move through major moments in the Bible, beginning with **Creation in Genesis** and ultimately progressing toward **Revelation**.

The game is built to:

- Encourage biblical knowledge
- Create an engaging faith-based gameplay experience
- Challenge players with Bible trivia and story decisions
- Offer both casual play and committed journey modes
- Present Scripture in a visually immersive and memorable way

---

## Core Gameplay

Players begin by choosing how they want to play:

### Quick Play
A simple entry mode for players who want to jump into the experience without a long-term save commitment.

### Journey Mode
A deeper story-driven mode where player progress, choices, and advancement are saved across sessions.

As players move through the game, they will:

- Travel through biblical eras and events
- Answer Bible trivia questions
- Unlock new stages of the journey
- Face consequences for incorrect answers based on the story context
- Strengthen their understanding of biblical history
